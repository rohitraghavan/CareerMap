What is iRate iSchool?

Career Map is an application to bridge the gap between future careers of iSchoolers and courses relevant to those careers . We realise that course selection can be really hard, so we built an app were you can rate and review courses listed by career concentration.

Why "Sign in with Linkedin"?

We want to ensure the quality of ratings and reviews. We believe that we can minimize bad behavior by ensuring that everyone's activities are tied to their professional identity on Linkedin.

What do you do with Linkedin data?

We only accesses your profile information (name, title, profile pic, etc.) and email address. We don't do anything with your data other than simply display it to our members. We don't sell your data, or use it for ads etc.
